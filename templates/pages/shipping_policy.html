Shipping Policy

Clear delivery timelines and tracking for every order.

Delivery Timeline

1. Standard shipping: 3 to 7 business days.

2. Priority shipping: 1 to 3 business days (selected locations).

3. Orders are processed Monday to Saturday, excluding public holidays.

Shipping Charges

Shipping cost is calculated at checkout based on destination, package weight, and selected delivery method. Free shipping may apply during campaigns or on qualifying order totals.

Tracking and Delays

1. Tracking details are shared by email once an order is dispatched.

2. Weather, courier operations, or remote locations may cause delays.

3. For delivery issues, contact: abrar.shop@example.com.
